1. Take 1/2 cucumber, 1 small carrot and 1/2 beetroot.
2. Wash them, peel them and cut the veggies into small pieces.
3. Put them into a mixer, add 3/4 water of the big glass and blend it for 1 minute at a speed of 2.
4. Strain the mixture into a glass.
5. Add 1/4 spoon of turmeric powder and mix it.
6. Done
